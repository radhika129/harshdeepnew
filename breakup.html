BREAKUP COACHING


Breakups are hard but they are not meant to break you. Breakups are there to break you free from your older self so that you can become a better version of yours. Numerous studies have shown people going through a breakup have symptoms or behaviors similar to drug withdrawal.
The breakups donâ€™t need to be painful and to be wasted months and years about the loss of a partner. A lot of people end up making a negative opinion about themselves or the opposite sex, it's time to heal quickly and use the power of breakup to unleash the amazing person within you.
A lot of times we have to work on past traumas or childhood traumas as well for people who get really hurt or are not able to move on, it happens because something in your past happened which created fear of abandonment unconsciously in you.


Phase 1- Goal setting and clarity coaching
Phase 2- Destroying triggers and Dissolving painful memories
Phase 3- working with grief, guilts and regrets (if any)
Phase 4- Past trauma and inner child healing
Phase 4- New daily routines and empowering alternatives
Phase 5- Weekly Follow-ups for 1 to 3 months (sometimes not required)
