BODY LANGUAGE


If you were looking to improve your personality and status you ended up in the right place. We all have heard don’t judge a book by its cover but we still end up judging people in an instance about their friendliness, dominance and potential as a sexual partner, most of it is impacted by non-verbal communication.



Have a look at this article from THE NEW YORK TIMES


“Like Mehrabian, he found that the verbal component of a face-to-face conversation is less than 35 percent and that over 65 percent of communication is done nonverbally. Our analysis of thousands of recorded sales interviews and negotiations during the 1970's and 1980's showed that, in business encounters, body language accounts for between 60 and 80 percent of the impact made around a negotiating table and that people from 60 to 80 percent of their initial opinion about a new person in less than four minutes.”



KEY AREA OF APPLICATION: Honestly, most aspects of your life.

  • Workplace/ Office
  • Sales
  • Relationships
  • General Communication
  • Public speaking
  • Personal branding

KEY HIGHLIGHTS:

  • Body Language
  • Conversational Hypnosis
  • Improve voice quality and tonality
 

I have mentioned in my bio that I was struggling with low self-esteem but still, I was able to do quite good in sales. It was majorly because of my strong body language and deep voice (chest voice). I use to come across as a confident person and a person with influence. If you are struggling with the same, I suggest you go through the transformational coaching first because the goal in life is to be genuinely happy not faking it. Don’t do mistakes like me!

When people ask me should they learn the art of an amazing body language, I give them this questionnaire: -

  • Do you want to turbo-boost your people’s skills?
  • Do you want to make better relationships?
  • Attract a better romantic partner?
  • Boost their sales and rapport with clients?
  • Do you want to dominate or get dominates?
  • Do you want to improve your communication with strangers and close ones?
  • Do you want to come across as confident, trustworthy and influencing person?
  • Hijack someone's mind without them even knowing?

I hope you got your answer.
I wish these things were taught in schools, I and a lot of us would have saved a lot of time but you have an option now, be wise enough!
