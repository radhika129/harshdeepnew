Harshdeep Singh

Mr Harshdeep Singh is an author, personal mastery coach and a graduate in Btech-CSE. Since childhood, he had an empathetic disposition to his personality. With immense love towards giving to the world, Singh has worked with animal rescue teams, NGOs, provided help in the community kitchens.

CLARITY-ON PURPOSE, Singh’s upcoming book talks about the mechanism of thinking. In the book, he explains the reasoning of thinking to attain clarification of life and provides tips for people to transform themselves resulting to which they can attain fulfilment.

At the age of 10 when kids worry about their stationery or uniform, Singh began a small yet amusing venture in deals. By the age of 18, Singh started working on his second business where he stepped into sales, networking and leadership roles.

Singh was slowly building his empire of dreams until one by one it all started to crash and fall out place. Singh was racially bullied for 10 years, had a toxic relationship, was facing business failure, all this lead to an implicit growth of depression. Singh felt lost, had lost his self-esteem & confidence and all these pushed Singh to even consider committing suicide.

One day, he decided that it’s time for him to face his problems, stop running from them and rediscover himself to grasp his life back. Commencing his journey of finding his purpose, Singh realised his love for helping others find clarification in their lives and reaching the state of fulfilment. The 360-degree turn in his life changed it all, he is now helping others find clarity, fulfilment and in turn contributing in nation-building.

Singh is an exceptional life coach determined to help others find clarity in life. Besides this Singh is also a fitness-enthusiast, a trainee in martial arts has interests in adventurous sports, music, reading, and exploring things. He is an animal lover, he adopted an indie dog who is very dear to him. They say that if a man loves a dog he is a good man, but if a dog loves a man he is definitely a good man. Singh is now a life coach and mentor he deserved but never had.
